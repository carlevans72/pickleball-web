Website Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2025

This Website Privacy Policy explains how PickleballFlow ("we", "our", or "us") handles information collected through the PickleballFlow website.

Mailing List Signups

The PickleballFlow website includes a mailing list signup form provided by MailerLite. If you choose to sign up, we collect the information you submit through that form, such as your email address and any other details requested in the form.

We use this information to send you updates about PickleballFlow, including product news, availability updates, and related announcements.

MailerLite

MailerLite processes mailing list data on our behalf. Information submitted through the signup form is handled by MailerLite under its own privacy and data processing terms.

You can learn more about MailerLite's privacy practices at MailerLite's Privacy Policy.

Cookies and Tracking

The MailerLite signup form may use cookies or similar technologies to provide the form, manage submissions, prevent abuse, and measure form performance.

We do not use website advertising trackers or sell website visitor data.

Unsubscribing

You can unsubscribe from PickleballFlow mailing list emails at any time by using the unsubscribe link included in those emails.

Deleting Your Information

If you would like us to delete your mailing list information, contact us at carl@pickleballflow.app.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Website Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
